When gathering these resources, I tried my best to review each form of content with sensitivity. However, I am just an 18-year-old high schooler and I still have a lot to learn through this process as well. The mission of 'Reform the Narrative' is to amplify voices, so I am willing to listen to your input and learn from them. If you have ANY concerns, questions, or suggestions, I encourage you to contact me through the form below or simply email me letsreformthenarrative@gmail.com. I want this platform to be collaborative and celebratory of different ideas. This is still a work in progress; there are still many resources that can be added to this site to generate inclusivity. If you have any suggestions or recommendations on articles, books, or other resources I can add, please feel free to contact me as well! I look forward to hearing from you!
