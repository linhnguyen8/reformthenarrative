To Organizations, Protesters, and Bailfunds

Black Lives Matter - a global organization whose mission is to eradicate white supremacy and build local power to intervene in violence inflicted on Black communities by the state and vigilantes


My Block, My Hood, My City - provides underprivileged youth with an awareness of the world and opportunities beyond their neighborhood


National Lawyer’s Guild - legal support in labor struggles, international rights monitoring, mass defense of political speech, rebuffing attacks on immigrants, women, people of color, LGBTQ persons, prisoners, and others facing government and institutional oppression


Color of Change - lead campaigns that take on the institution of policing head-on & advanced solutions for racial justice


Transgender District - lead the way to cure transgender houselessness, create an atmosphere that celebrates and affirms the ongoing presence, resilience, contributions, and culture of transgender people in San Francisco’s Tenderloin neighborhood, and ensure safe spaces for trans people that inspire economic advancement, leadership development and community


Black Trans Travel Fund- mutual aid based organization developed for the purpose of providing Black transgender women with the financial resources necessary for them to be able to self-determine and access their safest travel options


Unicorn Riot - dedicated to exposing root causes of dynamic social and environmental issues through amplifying stories and exploring sustainable alternatives in today’s globalized world


ACLU - The ACLU has been at the center of nearly every major civil liberties battle in the U.S. for over 100 years. They focus on issues such as Racial Justice, Criminal Justice, Women's Rights, LGBTQ Rights, and more(donate to your local ACLU chapter as well!)


The Bail Project - pay bail for people in need, reuniting families and restoring the presumption of innocence


Know Your Rights Camp - advance the liberation and well-being of Black and Brown communities through education, self-empowerment, mass-mobilization and the creation of new systems that elevate the next generation of change leaders


Loveland Therapy Fund - with the barriers affecting access to treatment by members of diverse ethnic and racial groups. Loveland Therapy Fund provides financial assistance to Black women and girls nationally seeking therapy


Split Donations to 70+ community bail funds, mutual aid funds, and racial justice organizers


Activist Medical Fund- donations will help pay the medical bills of activists in the Dallas-Fort Worth area who have been injured while protesting against police brutality
